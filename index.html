Hi, I'm Ramie

Hi there! I'm Ramie, currently a dedicated student at Oregon State University, pursuing a second Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. My journey into the world of Computer Science is fueled by a relentless passion for data analysis, programming, and software development. Having spent three years in the Finance industry, I've harnessed the power of data to drive business growth, streamline processes, and make informed decisions.

At Oregon State University, I'm immersed in a rich curriculum that includes courses such as Big Data Analytics, Data Structures & Algorithms, Discrete Structures, Operating Systems, Software Engineering, and Web Development. These subjects have not only honed my problem-solving skills but also equipped me with the technical prowess to excel in the ever-evolving world of data and technology.

My background in Business-Finance from DePaul University, where I graduated magna cum laude, laid a solid foundation in critical thinking and quantitative analysis. Alongside standard courses, I've been honored with numerous Dean's List awards and prestigious scholarships, such as the CFO, Fortis Investments, and Weber Scholarship.

In my professional journey, I've had the privilege of working with prominent institutions like Northern Trust, PNC Financial Services, and JPMorgan Chase. At Northern Trust, I served as a Senior Analyst in Hedge Fund Services, where I directed multifaceted reports and projects for a client with over $75 billion in AUM. I leveraged tools like Excel and PowerBI to provide data-driven recommendations, automated processes, resolved issues, and mentored junior colleagues.

My role at PNC Financial Services as an Analyst involved generating data-driven reports, conducting security policy audits, and facilitating a seamless M&A transition. It was here that I harnessed Tableau and Excel to deliver insights that resulted in a 20% increase in sales and ensured a 100% compliance rating with regulatory standards.

Beyond my professional endeavors, I'm eager to showcase my skills and creativity through a variety of personal projects. These include projects like Loan Data Cleaning and Transformation, Exploratory Data Analysis with Spotify Song Data, and Analyzing COVID-19 Impact, where I've employed SQL and Python to derive insights from complex datasets.

As I venture into the realms of data analysis, programming, and software development, I'm open to diverse roles, from Data Analyst to Data Engineer, Software Engineer, Software Developer, or Web Developer. My technical expertise spans Python, SQL, R, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a plethora of tools and platforms.

Feel free to explore my portfolio's Projects section for a closer look at my problem-solving skills and unique approach to data analysis. If you're interested in collaboration or wish to learn more about my work, please don't hesitate to get in touch. I'm excited about the limitless possibilities that the world of technology and data has to offer, and I'm committed to making a meaningful impact in this dynamic field.
